Welcome to Laxmi Farms

Three generations of passion, dedication, and expertise in cultivating the finest grapes in Nashik. Where tradition meets innovation.

Our Heritage

Founded in 1975, Laxmi Farms has been at the forefront of grape cultivation in the Nashik region. What started as a small family vineyard has grown into one of the most respected farms in Maharashtra. Our commitment to quality, sustainable farming practices, and innovation has made us a trusted name among grape connoisseurs across India.

Named after Goddess Laxmi, our farm embodies prosperity, purity, and abundance. Every grape that leaves our vineyard is a testament to our family's dedication and the rich soil of Nashik. We believe in farm-to-home freshness, ensuring that you receive the finest produce within 24 hours of harvest.

Our Journey

1975

The Beginning

Laxmi Farms was established with just 5 acres of land and a vision to grow the finest grapes in Nashik.

1995

Expansion Era

Expanded to 30 acres and introduced modern drip irrigation systems, setting new standards for sustainable farming.

2010

Organic Certification

Achieved 100% organic certification and won the "Best Organic Farm" award from Maharashtra Agriculture Department.

2020

Digital Transformation

Launched direct farm-to-home delivery service, bringing fresh grapes to customers within 24 hours of harvest.

Our Farming Philosophy

Water Conservation

Advanced drip irrigation systems ensure minimal water wastage while maximizing crop health.

Zero Waste

All farm waste is composted and returned to the soil, creating a sustainable ecosystem.

Solar Powered

100% of our farm operations are powered by renewable solar energy.

Community First

We provide fair wages and skill training to all our farm workers and their families.
